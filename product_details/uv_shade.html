UV Shade

( 3 Customer Reviews )
UV Shade Sunscreen Lotion is specially formulated with a combination of high-quality sunscreen agents, this lotion offers broad-spectrum protection against both UVA and UVB rays, shielding your skin from the harmful effects of sun exposure.
  • Descripton
  • Review (3)

    KEY BENEFITS:

    • Broad-spectrum SPF protection: Defends against both UVA and UVB rays to prevent sunburn, premature aging, and skin damage.
    • Water-resistant: Provides long-lasting protection, even during swimming or sweating.
    • Non-greasy formula: Lightweight and fast-absorbing, leaving your skin feeling smooth and comfortable.
    • Suitable for all skin types: Gentle enough for daily use on face and body.

HOW TO USE

Apply UV shade sunscreen lotion liberally & evenly on the face, neck and exposed area of skin 20 to 30 minutes prior to sun exposure. Further reapplication is only necessary after activities such as swimming or after excessive sweating.

KEY INGREDIENTS:

    Oxybenzone: Oxybenzone is a chemical UV filter that absorbs UVB and short UVA rays. It helps to prevent sunburn and reduce the risk of skin cancer.
    Octinoxate: Octinoxate is another chemical UV filter primarily used to absorb UVB rays. It's commonly found in sunscreens because of its effectiveness in preventing sunburn.
    Avobenzone: Avobenzone is a chemical UV filter that absorbs UVA rays. It's one of the most widely used UVA filters in sunscreens and provides crucial protection against long-wave UVA rays, which contribute to premature aging and skin cancer.
    Zinc Oxide: Zinc oxide is a physical UV filter that provides broad-spectrum protection against both UVA and UVB rays. It works by sitting on top of the skin and reflecting, scattering, and absorbing UV radiation. Zinc oxide is known for being gentle on the skin and suitable for sensitive skin types.
    Octocrylene: Octocrylene is a chemical UV filter that helps stabilize other UV filters and provides additional UVB protection. It's often used in combination with other active ingredients to enhance the overall efficacy of the sunscreen.
